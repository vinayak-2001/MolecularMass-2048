How to Play

  • Combine tiles of the same molecular mass of chemical compounds in an attempt.
  • Your aim is to create a tile with the molecular mass 2048 to won.
  • Pressing the arrow keys the game board will move all tiles in the direction of the key pressed.
  • Two tiles with the same molecular mass will merge when they collide to create a single tile with double the value.
  • Score updates with the molecular weights of two tiles which are combining.
  • The game is over when there are no valid ways for you to move the tiles.
